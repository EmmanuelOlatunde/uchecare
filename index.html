About The Initiative

Transforming Healthcare Delivery in Ondo State

UCHCECARE was established with a singular vision: to ensure that no resident of Ondo State is left behind when it comes to quality medical care. We bridge the gap between government resources and community needs.

From primary health centers in rural communities to our integrated digital pharmacy, we are making healthcare accessible, affordable, and reliable.

  • Subsidized treatments for registered members
  • Authentic, state-approved medications
  • 24/7 dedicated support via WhatsApp
